Mashed Potatoes

Description

Easy and quick recipe as a side for any meal!

Ingredients

  • 4 or 5 potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Cook the potatoes in water until they are soft
  2. Unpeel the potatoes and mash them with a fork or other utensil
  3. In a pan, add salt, a cup of milk and two spoonfuls of butter
  4. Add nutmeg to taste
  5. Stir until it has a uniform texture
  6. Add more milk to change its consistency if you need
