Apple Jello

Description:

This is an easy way to make some delicious dessert with only two ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 3 boxes of Jello, any flavor
  • 5 unpeeled, sliced apples
  • One cup of water

Steps:

  1. Wash, unpeel and slice the apples as you prefer
  2. Add the apples and Jello powder to a pressure cooker
  3. Add the water and turn the heat on
  4. Wait until the cooker starts to whistle, turn it off after five minutes
  5. Put it on a cooking dish and take it to the fridge until it's ready
