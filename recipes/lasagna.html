Lasagna

Description:

This lasagna recipe is simple yet delicious, made with only a few ingredients. It's a good option for family luncheons!

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Onion and garlic

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the following: meat sauce, noodles, meat sauce, cheese. Top with a cheese layer
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
